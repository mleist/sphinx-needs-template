# Risks

```{risk} Rain on party day
:id: RSK_RAIN_PARTY
:status: open
:tags: party_prep

Heavy rain on the planned party day would force the event indoors or
require last-minute tent rental. Mitigation: monitor the forecast 7 days
ahead and pre-book a tent if probability exceeds 40 %.
```

```{risk} Pest damage to vegetable bed
:id: RSK_PESTS_VEG
:status: open
:tags: planting

Aphids and slugs can decimate young tomato seedlings within days.
Mitigation: install copper barriers, use companion planting (basil),
and inspect plants every other morning.
```
