# Non-Functional Requirements

Constraints and quality attributes that apply across the project.

```{nfreq} Lawn-mower noise window
:id: NFR_NOISE_WINDOW
:status: accepted
:tags: mowing

Lawn mowing MUST happen between 09:00 and 18:00 on weekdays and between
10:00 and 18:00 on Saturdays. Mowing is forbidden on Sundays and public
holidays per local quiet-hours regulations.
```

```{nfreq} Vegetable-bed water budget
:id: NFR_WATER_BUDGET
:status: accepted
:tags: planting

Watering of the vegetable bed MUST not exceed 30 litres per square metre
per week during dry periods, to keep within the household's seasonal water
allowance.
```

```{nfreq} Party preparation lead time
:id: NFR_PARTY_LEAD_TIME
:status: accepted
:tags: party_prep

Preparation activities (mowing, trimming, planting) MUST begin no later
than 14 days before any planned garden party so that flower beds and lawn
have time to recover and bloom.
```
